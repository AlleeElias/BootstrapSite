What is Halo?

Halo is a billion dollar franchise that started in 1999 with the development for the original vision of Halo. It has since grown into a multibillion dollar franchise that has expanded into books, movies and TV-shows. After Bungies contract with Microsoft ended, Microsoft founded 343 Studios. Their purpose is to make Halo games. Most low level employees transitioned into 343 Studios and later on even high level employees left Bungie to rejoin their colleagues at 343 Studios.

Random facts

  • Halo was meant to be a MACOS game.
  • Development hell was common every game.
  • Bungie was never bought by Microsoft.
  • Halo Reach was made due to contractual obligations.
  • Every Halo game runs on a custom engine.
